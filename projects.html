Slice of Life


Slice of Life is a third person monster creation simulation game on PC. The objective is to go around each level collecting different body parts and assembling them to make your own unique monster. The more satisfied your clients are with your creation, the better your rewards.

This project was made using Unreal Engine version 4.23. I was the sole programmer for this project and I worked on all the core gameplay functionalities, including the body attachment/detachment system, the UI functionalities, the gacha system and more.

VR Carnival


VR Carnival is a VR PC game where you throw darts at a board to pop balloons. The balloons come in different sizes and points - the smaller ones are worth more points. The player's objective is to score as many points using the limited number of darts per round. The player can then spend their points to unlock prizes.

This project was made using Unity 2018. I was the sole programmer for this project and I worked on all the core gameplay functionalities, including the dart throwing, the points system and the prize system.

Island Defense


Engine: Unity 2018
Team: 6 people
Duration: 24 hours

Island Defense is a PC game where you defend your floating island tower against the endless of assault of meteors. The original plan was to allow the players to spend points for upgrades after each wave, but this feature was not implemented due to time constraints.

This was a game jam project made in 24 hours spread across 3 days using Unity 2018. The team consisted of 3 artists, 2 designers and myself as the sole programmer. My main task was to create the tower controls and the enemy spawner.
